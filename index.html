Hey, I'm Nicole, a front-end web developer and designer with an affinity for art, design, and all things aesthetic.

Making your daily venti soy matcha tea lattes and practicing the importance of “5, 6, se-ve-and-a, 8” used to be my day-to-day before the pandemic. Since then, coding and designing have become passions of mine and I soon came to realize that beautiful and accessible websites are what I strive to create, and my graduation from Juno College of Technology's Immersive Bootcamp has given me the tools I need to make that possible.
